Privacy Policy for CRM Fit & Master Bot
Last Updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Evgenii Pastushenko ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Telegram bots: CRM Fitness Bot and Master Bot (collectively, "the Bots"), which integrate with Google Calendar API.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Bots to access your Google Account, we collect:

Google Email Address: To identify your account and provide personalized service.

Google Calendar Data: We request access to view, create, edit, and delete events in your Google Calendar.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide the following functionality at the user's explicit request:

Syncing training sessions or work orders with your Google Calendar.

Creating, editing, or removing events based on your interactions with the Bot.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other purposes not related to the core functionality of the Bots.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access Tokens: We store OAuth refresh tokens to maintain the connection with Google Services.

Event IDs: We store only the unique IDs of the events created by the Bots to allow for future editing or deletion.

Encryption: All sensitive data (tokens) is stored in an encrypted database on our private Virtual Private Server (VPS).

Minimal Retention: We do not copy or store the contents of your other calendar events. We only interact with events managed by the Bots.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Selling/Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or Google user data to third parties.

Limited Use: Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can revoke the Bots' access to your Google account at any time through:

The Bot's internal settings (Disconnect Calendar).

Your Google Security Settings.
Upon disconnection, we will delete your access tokens from our database.
